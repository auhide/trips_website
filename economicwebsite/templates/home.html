Welcome to Trippy

This is a website that calculates the price of your car trips.

So how to create a trip?

Firstly, you'll have to SIGN UP and LOG IN. After that, open the CREATE A TRIP tab.

Then, you'll have to choose whether you want to travel locally or internationally.


This is how you have to enter the information for your trip results to be valid:

  • Countries (in English)
  • Towns or villages (in English)
  • Fuel cost (in Euro)
  • Fuel consumption (Litres per 100km, enter only the litres)

Finally, you'll have a personal list of trips in your MY TRIPS tab!
